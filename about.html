Our Vision

Communities where women are not defined by their past — but supported in building stable futures.

Est. 2014 · Norfolk, Virginia

About WE1A & Divinity House Support Services

WE1A (Women Empowering One Another) was founded on a simple belief: when women are given safe space, accountability, and support, transformation is possible.

Our approach

Executive summary ↗

Structured, trauma-informed programming within correctional institutions, focused on emotional regulation, accountability, and reentry readiness.

We partner with correctional facilities through consistent, contract-based programming that supports behavioral stability and reduces recidivism risk.

Our long-term vision expands a continuum of care through transitional housing and community-based support services.

What makes our approach different

Accountability

Women take ownership of their healing journey.

Emotional healing

We address trauma, addiction, and behavioral patterns at the root level.

Structured support

Balanced environments with compassion and responsibility.

Continuum of care

Support from incarceration to independent living.

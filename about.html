Our Mission

To empower women impacted by incarceration, addiction, and trauma through therapeutic support, personal development, and structured residential transition services.

Our Vision

Communities where women are not defined by their past — but supported in building stable futures.

Our Approach

WE1A delivers structured, trauma-informed programming within correctional institutions, focused on emotional regulation, accountability, and reentry readiness.

As part of a growing institutional strategy, we partner with correctional facilities to provide consistent, contract-based programming that supports behavioral stability and reduces recidivism risk.

Our long-term vision includes expanding a continuum of care through transitional housing and community-based support services.

About WE1A & Divinity House Support Services

WE1A (Women Empowering One Another) was founded in 2014 with a simple belief: When women are given safe space, accountability, and support, transformation is possible.

What Makes Our Approach Different

Accountability

Emotional Healing

Structured Support

Balanced environments with compassion and responsibility.

Continuum of Care

Support from incarceration to independent living.

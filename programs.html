Our Programs

Supporting Women Through Every Step of Recovery & Reentry

WE1A provides compassionate support for women impacted by incarceration, addiction, trauma, and life transitions. Our programs are designed to promote healing, stability, personal growth, and long-term success.

🏠

Recovery Support

Helping women build healthy foundations through guidance, accountability, peer support, and recovery-focused resources.

❤️

Trauma-Informed Care

Creating safe spaces where women can heal, rebuild confidence, and develop healthy coping strategies.

🎓

Life Skills Development

Workshops and training focused on personal growth, employment readiness, financial literacy, and goal setting.

🤝

Community Connection

Building meaningful relationships and support networks that strengthen recovery and long-term success.

Featured Initiative

Women Empowering One Another

Our signature approach centers on women helping women. Through mentorship, encouragement, education, and shared experiences, participants develop the confidence and skills needed to move forward with purpose.

Together we foster resilience, recovery, accountability, and hope.

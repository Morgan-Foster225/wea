Who We Work With

Human Trafficking Recovery

Survivor Ventures

📍 Virginia · Delaware · Florida — Virtual Delivery

Survivor Ventures provides comprehensive support to survivors of human trafficking across multiple states. WE1A facilitates individual and group sessions offering:

  • Trauma‑informed life skills development
  • Emotional regulation tools
  • Personal growth coaching
  • Goal‑setting and accountability
Life Transitions

F.I.A.B. (Friend In A Box)

📍 Community Spaces — In‑Person & Virtual

F.I.A.B. is a community‑centered program designed to support individuals navigating major life transitions. WE1A provides facilitation helping participants:

  • Build confidence and self‑awareness
  • Strengthen daily life skills
  • Develop healthy routines and boundaries
  • Create meaningful, achievable goals

What We Deliver

Services Offered Through These Partnerships

As a Certified Life Coach & Life Skills Facilitator, Charlotte Williams provides trauma‑informed support through:

Individual Coaching Sessions

Available in‑person or virtually, focusing on emotional wellness, personal development, and practical life skills tailored to each woman's journey.

Group Facilitation

Delivered in partnership with Survivor Ventures, F.I.A.B., and other community organizations. Group sessions include:

  • Emotional regulation & grounding techniques
  • Life skills development
  • Healthy boundaries
  • Self‑esteem building
  • Accountability & goal‑setting

Get Involved

At WEA1 and Divinity House, we believe real change happens when people come together with a shared purpose. Whether you want to give your time, share your skills, or support our mission, there are many meaningful ways to get involved.

Volunteer With Us

Volunteers are at the heart of everything we do. By giving your time, you help us expand our reach and deepen our impact in the community.

You can help by:

  • Assisting with community outreach events
  • Supporting program activities and workshops
  • Helping with administrative or organizational tasks
  • Mentoring individuals and families we serve

No matter your background or availability, there is a place for you here.

Partner With Us

We welcome partnerships with organizations, businesses, faith groups, and community leaders who share our vision.

Partnership opportunities include:

  • Co-hosting events and initiatives
  • Providing resources or services
  • Sponsoring programs
  • Collaborative community projects

Together, we can accomplish far more than we can alone.

Donate & Support

Financial support allows us to continue offering programs, maintaining safe spaces, and expanding services to those who need them most.

Your contributions help fund:

  • Community programs and outreach
  • Educational and support services
  • Facility needs and operations
  • Emergency assistance efforts

Every gift—large or small—makes a meaningful difference.

Join us at our gatherings, workshops, and community events. These are great opportunities to:

  • Connect with others
  • Learn about our mission
  • Participate in meaningful activities
  • Stay informed about ongoing initiatives

    • Share the Mission

    You can also support us by spreading the word. Follow us, invite others to events, and share our work within your networks.
